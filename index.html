Classic Cheesecake Recipe

A delicious and creamy cheesecake recipe.

If you're looking for a rich and indulgent dessert, cheesecake is always a crowd-pleaser. This classic recipe features a creamy, tangy filling and a sweet graham cracker crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 32 oz cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream, room temperature
  • Strawberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F (180째C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together the graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add 1 1/4 cups sugar and vanilla extract and beat until well combined.
  5. Beat in the eggs one at a time, making sure each egg is fully incorporated before adding the next.
  6. Add the heavy cream and beat until just combined.
  7. Pour the mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  8. Bake for 50-55 minutes or until the center is almost set.
  9. Allow the cheesecake to cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  10. Decorate with berries.
